Our Core Services

PET Bottle Collection

We collect used PET bottles from households, schools, streets, and local businesses to reduce plastic pollution.

Sorting & Aggregation

Collected bottles are cleaned and sorted into recyclable categories, preparing them for resale or further processing.

Recycling Education & Awareness

We organize community outreach and school programs to educate the public on the environmental and economic benefits of recycling.

Partnerships with Recycling Plants

We supply sorted PET bottles to recycling plants, where they are converted into reusable materials for new products.

In-house PET Processing Coming Soon

We plan to establish a local processing facility to convert PET bottles into flakes or pellets for local manufacturers.
